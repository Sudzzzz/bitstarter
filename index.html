Risks and challenges

With all things, I think I rage against the unforeseen. I try to plan, and strategize, and predict, and well foresee. Realistically, the only way to guard against production delays is to have multiple vendors capable of producing a quality product. Spend the time and energy to find more than just top search but find the company that is the best at what it does without making a $40 t-shirt.
